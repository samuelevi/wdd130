Nigeria

Lagos is Nigeria's bustling economic and cultural powerhouse, a massive coastal metropolis known for its vibrant energy, music (Afrobeats), Nollywood film industry, and diverse population, serving as Africa's largest city and a major financial hub despite its history as the former capital before Abuja. Spread across islands and a mainland, it's a dynamic, fast-growing megacity characterized by a humid climate, busy ports, and a unique blend of traditional roots and modern ambition, facing challenges like overcrowding but thriving as a center for commerce, innovation, and opportunity.
