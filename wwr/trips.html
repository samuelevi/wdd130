Featured Trips

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Extreme

Duration: Full Day (8 hours)

Description: Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on one of the most challenging rapids in the region. This trip is designed for experienced rafters seeking the ultimate white water adventure. Navigate through intense Class IV and V rapids while our expert guides ensure your safety.

Price: $150 per person

Big Mallard

Difficulty: Intermediate

Duration: Half Day (4 hours)

Description: Perfect for those with some rafting experience, Big Mallard offers a thrilling mix of Class II and III rapids. Enjoy scenic river views, wildlife spotting opportunities, and plenty of excitement. This trip strikes the perfect balance between adventure and accessibility.

Price: $85 per person

Half Day Adventure

Difficulty: Beginner

Duration: Half Day (3 hours)

Description: Ideal for families and first-time rafters, this gentle float features calm waters and beautiful scenery. Our guides will teach you proper techniques while you relax and enjoy the natural beauty of the river. Perfect for building confidence and creating lasting memories.

Price: $55 per person
